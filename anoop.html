Anoop Jacob

Minister for Food, Civil Supplies and Consumer Affairs

3 Departments

Anoop Jacob serves as the Minister for Food, Civil Supplies and Consumer Affairs in the Government of Kerala. He represents the Piravom constituency.

He oversees the distribution of essential commodities, consumer protection initiatives, public distribution systems and legal metrology services across the state.
