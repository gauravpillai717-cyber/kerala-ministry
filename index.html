About Kerala

Kerala is a state located on the south-western coast of India. It is widely known for its high literacy rate, rich cultural heritage, scenic landscapes and remarkable achievements in education and healthcare.

The Government of Kerala functions through the Legislative Assembly, Executive and Judiciary. The Legislative Assembly makes laws and discusses matters concerning the welfare and development of the state.

This portal provides information about ministers, their portfolios and their contributions to the governance and development of Kerala.
