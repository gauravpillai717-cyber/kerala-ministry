Mons Joseph

Minister for Water Resources and Housing

5 Departments

Mons Joseph serves as the Minister for Water Resources and Housing in the Government of Kerala. He represents the Kaduthuruthy constituency.

He oversees irrigation projects, groundwater development, water supply and sanitation initiatives, as well as housing development programmes aimed at improving living standards across Kerala.

DEPARTMENTS

  • Irrigation
  • Command Area Development Authority (CADA)
  • Ground Water Development
  • Water Supply & Sanitation
  • Housing

Constituency
