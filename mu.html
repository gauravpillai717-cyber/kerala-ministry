K. Muraleedharan

Minister for Health and Family Welfare & Devaswom

8 Departments

K. Muraleedharan serves as the Minister for Health and Family Welfare and Devaswom in the Government of Kerala. He represents the Vattiyoorkavu constituency.

He is responsible for strengthening healthcare services, medical education, food safety, indigenous medicine, and the administration of Devaswom institutions across the state.
