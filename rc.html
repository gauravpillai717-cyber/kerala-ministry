Ramesh Chennithala

Minister for index, Vigilance and Coir

4 Departments

Ramesh Chennithala is the Minister for index, Vigilance and Coir in the Government of Kerala. He represents the Haripad constituency and oversees law and order, vigilance administration, correctional services and the coir sector.

He plays a key role in ensuring public safety, transparency in administration and the promotion of Kerala's traditional coir industry.
