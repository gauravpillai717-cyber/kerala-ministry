V. D. Satheesan

Chief Minister of Kerala

V. D. Satheesan is the Chief Minister of Kerala and represents the Paravur constituency. He leads the Council of Ministers and oversees the administration and development initiatives of the state.

As Chief Minister, he is responsible for guiding policy decisions, coordinating government departments, and implementing welfare and development programmes.
