P. C. Vishnunadh

Minister for Tourism and Culture

5 Departments

P. C. Vishnunadh serves as the Minister for Tourism and Culture in the Government of Kerala. He represents the Kundara constituency.

He is responsible for promoting Kerala tourism, preserving cultural heritage, supporting film development initiatives and encouraging artistic and cultural activities throughout the state.

DEPARTMENTS

  • Tourism
  • Culture
  • Kerala State Film Development Corporation
  • Kerala State Chalachithra Academy
  • Kerala Cultural Activists Welfare Fund Board

Constituency

Kundara Assembly Constituency, Kerala
